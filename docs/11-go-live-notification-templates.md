## 1. Notification to Branch Manager Kitwe, Finance Director, and Director Operations

Subject: Southin Operations Hub – Controlled Go-Live for Stores, Assets, and Approvals

Dear Team,

We have started the controlled go-live phase for the Southin Operations Hub on the PeoplePay platform.

The portal is available here:

https://hub.southincon.com

This first go-live phase covers:

- Microsoft 365 staff login
- Site and warehouse master data
- Stores requisition capture
- Procurement request capture
- Approval inbox
- Stock location visibility
- Opening stock balance visibility

Approval Inbox:

https://hub.southincon.com/approvals/inbox

Kindly assist by reviewing requests routed to your level and advising where the workflow needs adjustment before wider rollout.

- Stock items

## 4. Notification to Site Managers

Subject: Southin Operations Hub – Site Manager Approval Role

Dear Site Manager,

You have been assigned as a site-level approver in the Southin Operations Hub.

Portal link:

https://hub.southincon.com

Please use the Approval Inbox to review requests assigned to your site:

https://hub.southincon.com/approvals/inbox

Before approving, confirm:

- The request belongs to your site.
- The item or service is required.
- The quantity is reasonable.
- The requester has provided a clear reason.

If the request is incorrect, reject it with a clear reason so it can be corrected.

Regards,  
Southin Systems Team
