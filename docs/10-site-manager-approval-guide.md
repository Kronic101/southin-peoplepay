# Site Manager Approval Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains how Site Managers approve operational requests in Southin PeoplePay / Operations Hub.

Site Managers are a control point before requests move to Branch Manager, Finance Director, Director Operations, or other senior approvers.

## Login

Open:

```text
https://hub.southincon.com

Login using Microsoft 365.

Where to Find Approvals
Open the portal.
Click Approval Inbox.
Review requests assigned to you or your role.
Open the request.
Check the request details before approving.
What to Check Before Approving

Before approving a Stores, Stock, Asset, Fleet, Leave, Overtime, or Timesheet request, confirm:

The employee or requester belongs to the correct site.
The request is for your site.
The requested item or action is operationally valid.
The quantity is reasonable.
The reason is clear.
The request is not duplicated.
The request should proceed to the next approval level.
When to Reject

Reject the request if:

It is assigned to the wrong site.
The request reason is unclear.
The quantity is incorrect.
The item is not required.
The request duplicates another request.
The requester must correct missing information.

Always enter a clear rejection reason.

Approval Flow

Typical stores approval flow:

Initiator raises request.
Site Manager reviews.
Branch Manager / Operations Manager reviews where applicable.
Finance Director reviews where applicable.
Director Operations gives final approval where required.
Stores processes the approved request.
Important Rule

Do not approve requests outside your assigned site unless you have been instructed to act as a delegated approver.

Email Notifications

Approvers may receive an email notification.

The email should direct the approver to:

https://hub.southincon.com/approvals/inbox

The approval action must be completed inside the system, not by replying to email.

Audit Requirement

Every approval decision records:

Approver name
Approver email
Role
Decision
Date and time
Comments or rejection reason

This protects the company during audits and payment/stock reviews.
